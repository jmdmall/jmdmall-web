Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, note the items you want, and send us a message via WhatsApp with your order details. We'll confirm and arrange delivery.

What are the payment methods?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and online payment methods. Details will be provided after you confirm your order.

What's the delivery time?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. We'll provide tracking information once your order is dispatched.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Contact us within 7 days of delivery for return requests.

Are products original and authentic?

Yes! All products are sourced from authorized dealers and manufacturers. We guarantee authenticity for all items sold on JMD Mall.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we deliver across India. Please contact us for international shipping inquiries.
